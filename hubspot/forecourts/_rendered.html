No Single Source of Truth

01 — Sensors

Automate the Collection of Multiple Data Sources

Wireless sensors collect operational data around the clock — replacing clipboards, spreadsheets, and guesswork across your forecourt.

Temperature

Fridges, freezers, hot holding cabinets, and food-to-go display cases — monitored 24/7 with automatic alerts when thresholds breach.

Humidity

Back-of-house storage areas and walk-in cold rooms where moisture levels affect stock quality and shelf life.

Door & Window

Cold room doors and freezer access points — detect when doors are left open, driving temperature excursions and energy waste.

Water & Leak Detection

Plant rooms, under-sink areas, and fuel pump bases — instant alerts on water ingress before it becomes a costly incident.

Motion & Occupancy

Customer toilets, back offices, and storage areas — trigger cleaning schedules or security alerts based on actual usage.

Touch

Manual check-in points and panic buttons — staff tap to confirm task completion or trigger an immediate escalation.

CO2 & Air Quality

Enclosed kitchen areas and back-of-house prep spaces — monitor air quality for staff safety and ventilation compliance.

Equipment Alarms

Generator alerts, HVAC faults, and compressor failures — relay monitoring that captures equipment status changes automatically.

Proximity

Asset presence detection for high-value displays, safety equipment, and compliance signage — know immediately when something is removed.
