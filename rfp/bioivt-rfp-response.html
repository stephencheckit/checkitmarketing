Why Checkit?

Checkit is a global leader in environmental monitoring. We understand how critical it is to have a strong partner, and we have built long-standing relationships in the environmental monitoring space with some of the largest plasma and laboratory customers in the United States. Checkit combines highly accurate monitoring with 24/7 alarm management, FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliant audit trails, and enterprise-wide visibility with long-term data integrity. We specialize in multi-site installations and employ our own in-house engineers who own the process from start to finish, including annual on-site calibrations.

These capabilities align closely with BioIVT's requirements for protecting high-value biospecimens, maintaining regulatory compliance, and ensuring operational continuity and efficiency across multiple laboratory and biorepository locations.

Our wireless temperature sensors are consistently shown to be extremely robust and accurate, with exceptionally low failure rates. Our sensors operate without maintenance and, in the rare event a replacement is necessary, can be easily replaced by lab staff. The solution is easy to operate and designed with frontline staff in mind.

Uniquely Able to Deliver

Checkit has no inventory, resourcing, or platform risks to delivery. We can guarantee adherence to the installation schedule that meets your needs and can complete the full deployment on your timeline. We employ our own trained engineers, staffed throughout the continental United States, who have a combined 50 years of installation experience in a variety of applications.

Compelling Commercial Offer

Checkit is unique in offering a full subscription-based proposal. Our subscription fee is inclusive of hardware, warranty, annual on-site calibration, and maintenance and support for the life of the agreement. Risk of hardware failure rests with Checkit, demonstrating confidence in the robustness of our technology, eliminating upfront capital investment, and providing transparency and certainty of future costs. Through this approach, Checkit accelerates time to value, maximises ROI, and minimizes risk.

Proven to Deliver at Scale

Checkit has delivered more than 800 successful installations in the United States, including many at significant scale with large enterprises such as Grifols, NAMSA, Quest Diagnostics, and the Center for Organ Recovery and Education. We provide temperature monitoring solutions covering a vast range of applications. Our specialty is multi-site organizations.

The Checkit system operates independently from your corporate IT infrastructure. Our sensors communicate via a dedicated wireless mesh network and do not traverse your corporate network, eliminating potential congestion and reliability issues.

Checkit offers 24x7x365 support and a nationwide network of skilled engineers. Our support and implementation capability is complemented by a dedicated customer success structure focused on building a long-term partnership.
